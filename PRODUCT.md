# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

A single Arabic-speaking individual managing their daily tasks, commitments, and self-accountability. The user is a self-directed person who wants to track what they've committed to, follow through daily, and hold themselves accountable without needing a coach or external enforcer. They open the app multiple times a day: morning to plan, during the day to check off, and evening to review.

## Product Purpose

Minhaj ("منهاج") is a personal discipline and task tracker focused on daily habits, commitments with penalties, and honest self-review. Success means the user opens it daily by habit and the friction is low enough that they never skip logging a task or penalty.

## Positioning

A fast, no-excuses daily discipline tool that gets out of your way. Not a project manager, not a social accountability app — just you, your tasks, and your commitments.

## Brand Personality

Serious, organized, reliable. The tone is direct and matter-of-fact, not cheerleading or punishing. The interface earns trust through predictability and speed.

## Anti-references

Not playful or childish. No bright cartoon colors, no rounded bubbly buttons, no bouncy animations, no gamification badges or rewards. The design should feel like a tool, not a game.

## Design Principles

1. Clarity over decoration — every pixel serves the task.
2. Speed as a feature — reduce taps, scrolling, and loading to zero friction.
3. Trust through consistency — same patterns everywhere, no surprises.
4. Accountability without burden — making a mistake should be straightforward to log, not shameful or confusing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. RTL-first layout. High contrast for readability in any lighting. The user may be checking tasks quickly in bright outdoor light or late at night — text must be legible in both.
